Applying for the membership of the Society: Please contact the ISUD Secretary.

You are also welcome to inform the ISUD Chair, in this cycle, Professor Robert Allinson; his email address is: Robert Allinson <rallinson@soka.edu>

Your short bio is required.

There is no entry fee.

The annual membership fee is 10 USD (American dollar), or equivalent, per year.

It is requested to pay the membership fee every two years: 20 USD or equivalent.

We accept the payment through the ISUD website.

The membership fee may be paid by PayPal. It can also be paid by credit card or bank transfer: for seeing more detail and/or for making payment, please, click DONATE
