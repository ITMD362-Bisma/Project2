ISUD Mission Statement

The ISUD is a society of scholars from many parts of the world, devoted to promoting international dialogue and the discussion of humanity's well-being. This includes:

  1. World peace.
  2. Human rights.
  3. Preservation of the world ecology.
  4. Dialogical interrelations of diverse cultures.

We take these universal concerns into a philosophical perspective, asking ourselves:

  1. What role does philosophical thinking have to play in moving toward a more rational and more humane future?
  2. What are ethical principles of dialogue?
  3. How may one understand cultural diversity and universality?
  4. How best to work toward a culture of peace?
  5. What is the relationship between the basic liberties of individuals and the collective rights of cultural groups?
